Yes. All ticket profiles grant native access to post-processed 4K session recordings, raw repository assets, and presenter slide decks within 24 hours of local session conclusions.

Passes are fully transferable up to 48 hours before the keynotes launch. Cancellations processed prior to 14 days before the event receive full structural refunds, while later cancellations receive digital-only access conversions.

Are there enterprise or structural discounts for large engineering teams?

Yes, tiered enterprise rates trigger automatically for teams booking 5 or more seats. Connect with our dedicated operations desk directly via the corporate portal to get custom volume invoicing pipelines configuration.
